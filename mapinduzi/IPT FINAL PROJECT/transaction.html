Dear costomer please follow the following instructions to pay via TIGO-PESA:

Tigo Pesa: Follow steps to pay

  1. Dial *150*01#
  2. Choose Option 4 --- "Pay Bills"("Lipia Bili")
  3. Choose Option 3 --- "Bussiness Number" ("Namba ya Kampuni")
  4. Enter Bussiness Number (Weka namba ya Kampuni) -- Namba ya Kampuni
  5. Enter Amount (Ingiza kiasi)
  6. Enter your PIN to confirm (Ingiza namba yako siri kuhakiki muamala)
